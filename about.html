About RentMate

RentMate is a college mini-project built by a team of 6 students. The idea is simple — students and everyday people often need to rent very different things (a room near college, a hostel bed, a hotel for a night, or a bike/cycle/car for a trip) but end up checking many separate apps and websites. RentMate brings all of these rental categories into one place.

Key features

🔍

Unified search

Search rooms, hostels, hotels, bikes, cycles and cars from one search bar.

⚡

Instant booking

Pick dates and confirm a booking in a few clicks.

↩️

Cancel anytime

Cancel upcoming bookings and get refunded to your wallet.

👛

RentMate Wallet

A simple in-app wallet for faster, cashless-style checkout.

📍

Map & location

See exact pickup/stay location for every listing before booking.

🪪

License/ID verification

Safety-first approach for vehicle pickups and stays.

⭐

Ratings & reviews

Real-style ratings to help you choose confidently.

🎓

Built for students

Designed with student budgets and campus locations in mind.

Project team

RentMate was designed and developed by a team of 6 students as part of a college project, covering UI/UX design, frontend development, and demo data logic.
