Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a room, hostel or vehicle? ⌄
Search by location and category on the home page, open a listing, pick your dates and confirm booking using RentMate Wallet or UPI.
Can I cancel my booking? ⌄
Yes. Go to My Account → Bookings and click Cancel on any upcoming booking. The amount is refunded to your RentMate Wallet instantly.
Is license verification required for bikes and cars? ⌄
Yes, a valid driving license must be shown at pickup for all bike and car rentals, as part of our safety policy.
What is RentMate Wallet? ⌄
It's an in-app wallet for faster checkout. Refunds and cashback are credited here, and you can use it to pay for any booking.
How are listings verified? ⌄
Every room, hostel, hotel and vehicle listing is checked for accuracy and safety before it appears on RentMate.
I'm a student — are there special prices? ⌄
Yes, many listings near colleges offer student-friendly pricing, and app-only offers give extra discounts.
